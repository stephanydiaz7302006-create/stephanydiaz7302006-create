Career overview: Marketing in Skincare 


Skincare marketing is a career focused on promoting and selling skincare products such as cleansers, serums, moisturizers, sunscreens, and treatments. The people in this field work on branding, social media campaigns, advertising, influencer partnerships, product launches, and understanding customer behavior. Skincare overall helps everyone around the world. 

AI disruption:
The AI could significantly change skincare marketing by doing social media posts, designing basic visuals, and analyzing customer data. AI tools can also cause beauty trends and consumer preferences, reducing the need for large marketing teams. Some roles like content creation or basic market research may become easier for AI to take over. 

AI benefits:
AI can help skincare marketers work more efficiently and make better choices. This can analyze large amounts of customer data to identify trends, improve targeting for ads, and marketing messages for different audiences. AI can also help create ideas,improve communication with customers through chatbots, automated responses, and much more. This should help marketers to focus on strategy and creativity rather than multiple tasks.

Gaining an advantage:
Entering skincare marketing can gain knowledge on how to use AI tools. For example like content creation, social media management, and data analysis. This should be able to use AI to create marketing campaigns faster, test different ad styles, and understand which strategies perform best. They can also learn prompt writing to get better outputs from AI tools and use AI analytics tools to make smarter marketing decisions. Being able to combine creativity with AI skills will make someone more valuable than others who only rely on different ways. 

Action plan:
 - Learn how to use AI marketing for example ChatGPT, Canva AI, or similar platforms to create content and ads.  
-  Practice analyzing skincare trends on social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram using AI tools or insights.  
- Take an online course in digital marketing or social media marketing to feel conformable about the knowledge.  
-  Create a small mock skincare brand and use AI to design a full marketing campaign for practice.
